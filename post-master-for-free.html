How to Master a Track for Free Using VST3 Plugins

Achieve commercial loudness and clarity without expensive mastering suites.

Mastering doesn't have to be a dark art that requires thousands of dollars in analog outboard gear. With the right free VST3 plugins and a trained ear, you can get your tracks sounding loud, wide, and clear enough for Spotify and Apple Music.

The Essential Free Mastering Chain

Every solid mix and master starts with good EQ and ends with controlled limiting. Here is the order you should follow:

  1. Surgical EQ: Use a transparent EQ like TDR Nova to cut out resonant frequencies.
  2. Bus Compression: Use Analog Obsession's FET compressors to glue the mix together.
  3. Dynamic EQ: Duck harsh frequencies only when they get too loud.
  4. True Peak Limiter: Push the loudness while preventing digital clipping.
